Product Details

Martell VSOP cognac is a golden-amber appearing, smooth and mellow cognac. V.S.O.P in France means Very Special Old Pale. It is skillfully blended with mature Eaux-de-vie from the finest terroirs of the cognac region of France. Martell VSOP is a cocktail of aromas with the bittersweet softness of lime and liquor ice, crystallized fruit notes of quince, raisin, and plum, and spring undergrowth notes of fresh notes of oak wood.
